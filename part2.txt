### 2. 🆘 EMERGENCY CONTACTS (always free)

**UI: Red SOS button in country header, always visible.**

Content per country:

**Emergency Numbers (large, tappable tel: links):**
- 🚨 General Emergency: 112
- 👮 Police: 110 (DE) / 101 (BE) / 999 (UK) / 000 (AU)
- 🚒 Fire: 112 (DE) / 100 (BE)
- 🚑 Ambulance: 112 (DE) / 100 (BE) / 999 (UK)
- ☎️ Non-emergency police: varies

**Medical After-Hours:**
- Weekend/night doctor service:
  - 🇩🇪 Germany: Bereitschaftsdienst — call 116117
  - 🇧🇪 Belgium: Wachtpost / Huisarts van wacht — call 1733
  - 🇳🇱 Netherlands: Huisartsenpost — call your GP's after-hours number
  - 🇬🇧 UK: NHS 111
  - 🇫🇷 France: SOS Médecins — call 3624
  - 🇦🇺 Australia: Healthdirect — 1800 022 222
- How to access: "Call first, they will direct you to the nearest location"
- Hours: "Weekdays after 5pm, weekends and holidays"

**Other Emergency:**
- ☠️ Poison control: country-specific number
- 🧠 Mental health crisis: country-specific
- 🏠 Domestic violence: country-specific
- 👶 Child protection: country-specific
- 🚗 Road assistance: ADAC (DE), ANWB (NL), RAC (UK), AAA (US)
- ⚡ Electricity emergency: company-specific
- 🔥 Gas emergency: company-specific
- 💧 Water emergency: municipality-specific

**How to call 112:**
- Works from any phone, even without SIM card
- Free of charge
- State: your location, type of emergency, number of people affected
- Stay on the line until instructed otherwise

## EMAIL REMINDERS

### Trigger Logic
Daily cron (Supabase Edge Function or Vercel Cron):
1. Query all users with `notifications_enabled = true`
2. For each user, generate their checklist (country + profile)
3. Calculate due dates: `arrival_date + task.deadlineDays`
4. Find tasks that are:
   - Due in 7 days AND not yet reminded (7day)
   - Due in 1 day AND not yet reminded (1day)
   - Due today AND not yet reminded (today)
5. Send email via Resend
6. Log to `reminder_log` to prevent duplicates

### Email Template
```
Subject: ⏰ [Task title] — due in [X] days in [Country]

Hi [name],
```
